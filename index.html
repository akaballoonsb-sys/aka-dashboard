Permit to Fly Guidelines

Annual Flight Readiness Inspections

Under Civil Aviation Safety regulations, every passenger-carrying hot air balloon must pass an annual inspection. This comprehensive checklist includes envelope tensile strength testing, burn testing, high-temperature indicator validation, and burner system pressure tests. The "Permit to Fly" must be renewed prior to its expiration date; failure to do so results in the immediate operational suspension of the aircraft.

Certificate of Registration (CofR)

Biennial National Registry Audits

The Certificate of Registration validates legal nationality and structural ownership status flags. Unlike the airworthiness permit, the CofR runs a 24-month cyclical schedule focused on registration ownership validation, weight and balance modifications, and logbook recording continuity. Operating an airframe with an expired CofR triggers an automatic registry invalidation flag.
